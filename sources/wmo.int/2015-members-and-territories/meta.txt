Description:
WMO Data in machine readable format.
[ISO3](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1_alpha-3) country codes
are used as identifiers.
Use [this tool](https://www.wmo.int/cpdb/tools/countrymatcher) to match
Country Names to their ISO3 codes.
