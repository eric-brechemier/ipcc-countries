Description:
There are 197 sovereign states in the world.
Country subdivisions and the smallest map units.

Medium scale data, 1:50m
Suitable for making zoomed-out maps of countries and regions.
Show the world on a tabloid size page.

1:50,000,000
1″ = 790 miles
1 cm = 500 km

Map units: Not usually useful but some FIPS and ISO codes
refer to groups of individual admin-0 territories by these designations.
US Pacific Island Territories, French Guiana, and more.
